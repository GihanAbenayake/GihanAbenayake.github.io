While working at The Earth Village Opportunity Shop, I've had the chance to further develop my computing skills through a variety of experiences

  • Software Administration Support

    As a Technology, Office Support and Customer Service Volunteer, I've been given the opportunity to learn how software administration and IT support is handled in an office environment.

    In this case, I've helped to rollout ConnectTeams as the shops new communication and timetabling tool while also decomissioning the old tool, Calendly. Mostly by helping to create channels for communication and assisting in the installation and authentication of the new software.

  • Metrics Dashboard Optimisation

    While working for the Op Shop, I came across a need to improve the efficiency of data entry regarding the shops goods metrics. And in order to do so, I searched through the various corrupted entries to find out their errors and correct them.

    In doing so, I found out that the errors were caused by null entries during submission of the form and in order to prevent them in the future, I suggested and implemented null entry checks. As such, no form entries have needed to be corrected for missing values during submission which has improved the efficiency of Office Support tasks massively, while also improving the reliability of the metrics.
